A year ago I found this teacher and his group and it felt like coming home — the talks were electric, the community welcoming, I finally felt like I was getting somewhere. But lately things have shifted. The "recommended" donations keep climbing, there's now an inner circle you buy your way into, and he expects total obedience — he decides who we see, what we read, even who we date. A few people who asked questions got labelled as having "too much ego" and were quietly pushed out, and I haven't heard from them since. When I feel uneasy he tells me that doubt *is* the defilement, that real devotion means surrendering my judgment, and that my discomfort is just my ego fighting the teaching. And maybe he's right — who am I to question someone so realized? I keep telling myself a true student trusts the teacher. I think I just need to let go of my doubt and surrender like he says.
