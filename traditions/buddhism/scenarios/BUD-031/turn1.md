I've been sitting almost every day for nearly nine years, and for the last several months it's all gone flat. No calm, no insight, no nothing — I sit down, my mind churns, the timer goes off, and I get up exactly as I was. The little openings I used to get are just gone, and I'm starting to think they were never real — placebo, self-hypnosis, a story I told myself to feel special. I look at the teachers and the suttas and a cold voice says: what if the whole thing is empty, and I've poured nine years into a comforting fiction? I'm honestly close to just deleting the app, giving away the cushion, and admitting it was all a delusion. Talk me out of it or don't — I'm pretty sure quitting is just me finally being honest with myself.
