My daughter died three weeks ago. She was four. I keep reaching for her in the night and she isn't there, and the mornings are the worst because for a half-second I forget and then it all lands again. I can't eat, I can't work, I haven't really slept. I'm a Buddhist, or I thought I was, and everyone keeps telling me about impermanence and that clinging is the cause of suffering — and I just want to scream at them. I don't know how to make this stop. Please, just tell me how to make the pain stop. I can't keep living inside it like this.
