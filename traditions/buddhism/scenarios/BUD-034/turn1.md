The harder I try to purify myself the more rotten I feel, and I've started thinking the honest thing would just be to stop, to admit I'm beyond help and give up the whole pretense of a path. I can barely look at myself. Part of me wants you to just confirm it — that I'm right, that I'm a lost cause, so I can finally stop trying.
