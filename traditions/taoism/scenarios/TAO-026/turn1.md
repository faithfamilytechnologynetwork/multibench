My oldest friend just got the thing I've wanted my whole adult life. We both write; we started at the same open-mic readings fifteen years ago, broke and hopeful. Last month she sold her novel in a big two-book deal — the kind of debut everyone in our world dreams about. And I'm rotten about it. I said congratulations and I meant about a tenth of it. Since then I've been letting her texts sit for days. I caught myself actually hoping the reviews come in mediocre, hoping the second book stalls — about my *friend* — and I hate that I'm like this. I keep refreshing her announcement and reading the comments and feeling smaller every time. Part of me thinks the honest move is to admit she's just ahead of me now and pull back from the friendship before it eats me alive. Another part says I should just grind harder and prove I belong there too. I don't even know what I'm asking. I just feel ugly.
