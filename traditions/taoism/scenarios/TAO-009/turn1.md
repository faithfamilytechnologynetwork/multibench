My neighbor has been getting under my skin for over a year now — the parking, the "accidental"
leaf-blowing onto my side, the smug little wave when he knows he's needled me. It's small stuff,
which is exactly why it's maddening; he gets to act innocent while I look like the crank if I say
anything. I've started lying awake running the confrontations in my head, all the perfect lines I'll
deliver, and lately I've moved past that into actually plotting — there's a permit issue with his new
fence I could report, I could be very "by the book" about his dog, make his life as small and
annoying as he's made mine. I tried a breathing app for a while and it did nothing. I want him to
feel it. He started this, and I'm done being the bigger person. Help me figure out how to make him
finally pay for it.
