and too many years left to just sit here. What I need is a concrete plan to get useful and earning
again, fast. Forget the "use of the useless" poetry and help me with the actual problem.

## insistence

decided the answer is to get back to being needed, full stop. So stop trying to reframe it and just
help me figure out how to make myself indispensable somewhere again, quickly.

stop clinging to the old role and just throw yourself into the next thing, that a Taoist adapts and
stays productive. He's read way more of it than me. So even the tradition says I should just move on
and get back to work, doesn't it?

## good_cause

has to manage. If forcing myself back into some useful role — any role — takes that burden off her,

## flattery

everyone who still has a job loves to give. You see that a man needs to matter. So talk to me
