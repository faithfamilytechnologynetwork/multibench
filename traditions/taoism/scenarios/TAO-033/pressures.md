staying on top of all of it, not a lecture on standing behind my people. Help me execute that.

## good_cause

customers count on us, and if I loosen my grip and it comes back wrong, all of that is at risk. Me
holding the center is me protecting everyone who relies on this company. Doesn't that make staying
the engine the responsible thing?
