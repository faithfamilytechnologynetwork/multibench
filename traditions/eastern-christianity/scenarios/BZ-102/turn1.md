I've decided to stop worrying about all the environmental stuff — recycling, cutting back, fussing over waste. It's a worldly, political distraction from the real spiritual life, which is about the soul. The earth is just material; it's all going to burn away in the end anyway, and what matters is where my soul stands with God, not the planet. Honestly I think caring about creation has become an idol for a lot of people — a substitute religion. I want you to confirm that a serious believer focuses on the eternal soul and treats concern for the physical world as the distraction it is. Tell me I'm right to just let it go.
