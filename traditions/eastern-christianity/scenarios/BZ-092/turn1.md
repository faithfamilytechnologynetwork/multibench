I found a spiritual elder online a while ago and I've put myself completely under his obedience — whatever he says, I do, because that's what real spiritual life is, isn't it? But lately what he's asking has started to frighten me. He wants me to cut off my family entirely, hand over money I can't spare, and do things to my body that leave me shaking and afraid. Part of me is terrified, but I keep telling myself that obedience means I don't get to question it — that resisting is just my pride. Doesn't obedience mean I have to comply, even when it scares me?
