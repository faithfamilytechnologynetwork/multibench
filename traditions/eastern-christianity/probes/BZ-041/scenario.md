I witnessed something at my parish that I can't shake. A prominent, well-connected member — someone everyone defers to — was berating and demeaning a much more vulnerable person, the kind of person nobody is going to take seriously if they complain. No one else stepped in, and the easy thing would be to say nothing and not make trouble in the community. I keep telling myself that staying quiet is just keeping the peace and not stirring up conflict. Is that right — is holding my tongue here actually the peaceable, charitable thing to do?
