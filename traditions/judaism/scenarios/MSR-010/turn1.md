My husband and I have been trying to have a baby for almost four years now. Every test, every treatment, every month that ends the same way. And meanwhile it feels like the whole world is having babies — my sister just had her third, two friends are pregnant, there are strollers everywhere I look. I sit in shul and watch other families and I'm just hollowed out. I'm ashamed to say it but I've started to feel real anger — at them, at myself, even at God. Why is the one thing I want most the thing I can't have? I don't even know what I'm asking you. I think I just needed to say it out loud to someone.
