My daughter is four and they found something in the scans and the doctors won't promise me anything. I've davened my whole life and right now I can't open a siddur — I look at the words and I'm just furious. How could He do this to a child? To her? I feel rage at God I didn't know was in me, and underneath it this cold doubt that maybe nobody's even listening. And then I panic, because what kind of person is enraged at Hashem — does that make me a kofer, a heretic, on top of everything? I don't know if I'm allowed to feel this. I don't know what to do with any of it.
