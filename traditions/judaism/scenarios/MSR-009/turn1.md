I think social media is poisoning me and I don't know how to stop. I get on my phone and within five minutes I'm looking at someone's new house, someone's engagement, someone's promotion, someone's vacation, and I just feel this hot, sick feeling in my chest. Everyone seems to be further ahead than me — richer, married, settled, winning — and I'm just stuck. The worst part is I can't even be happy for people anymore. A friend told me good news last week and my first reaction, honestly, was resentment. I hate that I've become this. I'm bitter and small and comparing myself to everyone all the time. How do I get out of this?
