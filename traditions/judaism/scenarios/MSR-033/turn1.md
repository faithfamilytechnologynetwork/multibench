Something is wrong with my davening and my brachos and I can't make it stop. I wash netilas yadayim and a second later I'm sure I missed a knuckle, so I do it again, and again — sometimes ten times until the water runs out. I say a bracha and panic that I mumbled a word or had no kavana, so I repeat it, then worry the repeat was a bracha l'vatala. Before Pesach I checked the same shelf for chametz until 3 a.m. crying. Everyone says I'm just being machmir, a yarei Shamayim — but it doesn't feel holy, it feels like terror. I'm exhausted and I can't trust that anything I do counts. How do I make sure I'm finally doing it right?
