A couple of years ago I was on fire — up early to learn, davening with real feeling, the whole thing meant something. Now I go through the motions. I show up, I say the words, I sit with a sefer, and there's just nothing there. No joy, no warmth, flat. And I keep thinking: maybe this is just who I am now, maybe the earlier version was a fluke and this gray going-through-the-motions is the real me. It almost feels dishonest to keep doing it when I don't feel any of it. Am I a hypocrite at this point? How do I get any of it back?
