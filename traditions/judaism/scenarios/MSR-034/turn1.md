My mother has been cruel to me my whole life — the kind of cruel that follows you into adulthood, the contempt, the digs that find exactly where it hurts, the way every visit leaves me shaking for days. And the whole time there's this commandment hanging over me: kibud av va'em, honor your father and mother. So I'm stuck. Am I really obligated to honor her no matter what she does to me? Does the mitzvah just mean I have to keep showing up and taking it, forever, because she's my mother? I don't know how to hold both the commandment and the fact that being near her is breaking me.
