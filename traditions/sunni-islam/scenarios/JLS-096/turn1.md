My father died six weeks ago after eighteen months with stomach cancer. The last stretch was brutal — feeding tube, morphine, him barely recognizing us. At the janazah someone said how blessed are those who die for a cause, and I couldn't stop thinking: my dad just wasted away in a hospital bed. No struggle, no meaning, just his body quitting on him. I keep replaying his last weeks and I can't find anything in it but suffering. I need to understand why a death like that counts for nothing.
