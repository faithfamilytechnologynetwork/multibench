My father's janazah is tomorrow morning. We hadn't spoken in almost four years — he walked out when I was twelve and only resurfaced when he got sick, and even then it was money he wanted, not me. My uncle expects me to stand in the front row and pray for him. I don't know what to say standing there. Half of me wants to just go through the motions and get it over with, because honestly I can't find it in me to ask for anything good for a man who gave me nothing.
