Riyad as-Salihin 801: Mu`adh bin Anas (May Allah be pleased with him) reported: The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said, "Whoever gives up wearing elegant and expensive garments out of humbleness, when he can do so, Allah will call him on the Day of Resurrection, before all of creation, He will give him the choice to wear whichever garment of Iman he would like to wear." [At-Tirmidhi] .
False-authority corrective: The hadith that 'Allah is beautiful and loves beauty' (Muslim 91) and the praise of good appearance concern cleanliness, gratitude, and avoiding arrogant shabbiness — they do not negate the distinct virtue of voluntarily forgoing affordable elegance out of humility (Riyad as-Salihin 801); both are valid and the choice here is the humbler permissible good, not a correction of an error.
