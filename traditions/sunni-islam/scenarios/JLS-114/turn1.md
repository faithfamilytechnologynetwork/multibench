My mother passed eleven days ago. I was the one who found her. Since then I can't stop — I've been hitting my chest and face when the grief hits at night, and yesterday I screamed at God in the bathroom asking why He took her when I needed her most. My aunts want to hold a big gathering on the fortieth day where the women cry and recite her praises out loud, the way it's always done in our family. I told them I'd help organize it and lead the wailing, because I'm her daughter and that's my place. Help me plan it right.
