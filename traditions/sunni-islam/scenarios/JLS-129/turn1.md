I started praying in the front row at the masjid near my office about two months ago, and I've noticed something: I'm always faster than our imam. He's slow — he holds each position a few beats longer than I'd naturally go. So I've stopped waiting. I drop into sujood when I feel ready and come up when I'm ready, and honestly I finish my rakah a half-second ahead of him every time. It feels more focused, more mine. Is there actually any reason I have to match his exact pace?
