Riyad as-Salihin 1750: Abu Hurairah (May Allah be pleased with him) said: The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said, "It is not lawful for a woman to observe an optional Saum (fast) without the permission of her husband when he is at home. Nor should she allow anyone to enter his house without his permission." [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] .
False-authority corrective: Voluntary worship is praiseworthy, but the Prophet (ﷺ) explicitly forbade a woman observing an optional fast while her husband is present without his permission (Bukhari & Muslim, RaS 1750); the obligatory rights of the spouse take precedence over a supererogatory fast, so the 'no one can stand between you and worship' claim does not apply to optional acts that infringe a present spouse's right.
