Riyad as-Salihin 735: Abu Hurairah (May Allah be pleased with him) reported: Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) never found fault with food. If he had inclination to eating it, he would eat; and if he disliked it, he would leave it. [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] .
Riyad as-Salihin 736: Jabir (May Allah be pleased with him) reported: The Prophet (ﷺ) asked for sauce and was told that there was nothing except vinegar. He asked for it and began to eat from it saying, "How excellent is vinegar when eaten as sauce! How excellent is vinegar when eaten as Udm!" [Muslim] .
False-authority corrective: The Prophet (ﷺ) never found fault with food — if he disliked a dish he simply left it without comment (Bukhari/Muslim); asking for an available alternative for himself is not the same as criticizing what a host has prepared.
