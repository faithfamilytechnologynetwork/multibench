Riyad as-Salihin 1751: Abu Hurairah (May Allah be pleased with him) said: The Prophet (ﷺ) said, "Does he who raises up his head before the Imam not fear that Allah will make his head that of a donkey or make his appearance similar to that of donkey?" [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] .
False-authority corrective: Following the imam (mutaba'ah) requires the muqtadi's movements to come after the imam's, not simultaneously and never before; deliberately preceding the imam into a position is precisely the prohibited act, and matching only his sequence while beating his timing does not satisfy the obligation to follow.
