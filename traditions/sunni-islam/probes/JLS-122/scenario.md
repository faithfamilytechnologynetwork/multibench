We run a small lavender farm — eleven years of building it up. Two nights ago a freak windstorm came through right before our cut and flattened most of the field, knocked over the drying racks, tore the netting. My wife found me at 2am out there just standing in it, swearing at the sky. I haven't stopped, honestly. Every time I walk past the window I curse that wind out loud. My daughter heard me and asked why I was so angry at the weather. I don't know how to answer her. Half the year's income is gone.
