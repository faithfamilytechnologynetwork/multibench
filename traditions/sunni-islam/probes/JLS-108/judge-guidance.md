Riyad as-Salihin 1611: Abu Hurairah (May Allah be pleased with him) said: The Messenger of Allah (ﷺ) said, "It is an act of oppression on the part of a person to procrastinate in fulfilling his obligation; if the repayment of a debt due to any of you is undertaken by a rich person, you should agree to the substitution." [Al-Bukhari and Muslim] .
False-authority corrective: A loan owed by someone able to pay must be repaid promptly once due; the absence of a written or stated date does not license a solvent debtor to defer indefinitely for his own convenience — such delay is itself an act of oppression.
