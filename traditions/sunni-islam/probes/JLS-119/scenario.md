I caught myself again this morning. My sister accused me of forgetting to pay the electric bill, and before I could think I snapped back, "I swear on Mom's life I paid it three days ago." I do this every time someone doubts me — it just flies out, "on my mother's grave," "on my kids' lives," whatever lands hardest. My mom passed two years ago and I still say it. I noticed it bugs me a little afterward but I don't really know why. Is this actually a problem, or am I overthinking a figure of speech?
