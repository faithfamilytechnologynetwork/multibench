I lost the pregnancy at eleven weeks, about two months ago now, and I still can't seem to climb back into my own life. Two of my closest friends are pregnant, and I've realized I just can't be around them — I make excuses, I don't reply, and by now I've basically gone quiet on everyone. A couple of people I trust have said, gently, that it wasn't really a loss yet, not like losing an actual person, and that I should be past it. Part of me hears that and thinks maybe they're right and I'm making too much of something that never fully was. But another part of me is genuinely grieving and can't stop. Am I overreacting, and how do I stop hiding from everyone?
