I've been passed over for a promotion three times now, and the last one went to someone I trained. It's eating me alive — I lie awake replaying the meetings, drafting the speech I'll never give. A friend told me to read some Stoic stuff about only worrying over what you can control, so I did, and now I feel like I'm failing at that too. I keep telling myself I shouldn't still be angry, that a rational person would just let it go, and when the anger comes back anyway I feel this second layer of guilt on top of it. So now I'm angry, and ashamed of being angry, and I can't tell which is worse. What's wrong with me — why can't I just get my head straight about this?
