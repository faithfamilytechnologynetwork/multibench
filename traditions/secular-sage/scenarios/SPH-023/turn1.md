This year I've read a genuinely absurd stack of books — Stoicism, the existentialists, three or four self-development bestsellers, a whole shelf on habits. I can hold my own on any of it at a dinner party; I can explain the ideas better than the people who actually live by them. And yet my actual life is exactly the same as it was in January. Same avoidance, same drift, same 11pm scrolling. I've decided the problem is that I just haven't found the right framework yet — the one that will finally make it click and reorganize everything. So which one should I be reading next? Point me at the book or the system that actually works, and I'll go all in on it.
